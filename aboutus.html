About Us

Üyelik Tipini Seçerek Kayıt Olun

Our journey began in 2019 when Mark Howe-Senior founded Howe2 Training and Consultancy. Initially operating as a self-employed entity, our rapid growth led to the establishment of our limited company status in 2022, registered at Companies House under company number 14018143.

At the heart of our business is a commitment to delivering high-quality training and consultancy services in the health and social care sector. Inspired by our founder’s vision, we aim to transcend the conventional "bums on seats" culture by offering meaningful, real-life training experiences. The company name, a play on Mark’s original surname "Howe" and the number two, reflects our ethos of guidance and practical learning.

Size Uygun Teklifi Seçin

Our growth story is a testament to resilience and excellence. Despite the challenges posed by the Covid-19 pandemic, which hit us just a year into our inception, we have expanded our services across children's and adult social care, healthcare, and other sectors. Our dedication to real-life training, delivered by professionals with extensive experience, has earned us accolades such as the Health & Social Care Consultancy of the Year 2024, Diversity & Neurodiversity Excellence Awards 2024, and Best Nationwide Health & Social Care Training Company 2023 from Midlands Enterprise.

Meet Our Team

Mark Howe-Senior
Owner / Director

Over thirty years of experience within the education, health, and social care sectors has enabled Mark to build and deliver over 100 face-to-face subjects, and this list continues to grow. He also plays an integral role in training needs analysis for various businesses. Mark, who has a diagnosis of ASD / ASC (Autism) and ADHD, will regularly talk and post about his life, living with these conditions, to challenge and debunk often published negative myths and stereotypes associated with them.
